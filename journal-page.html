Record Your Thoughts, Capture Your Journey

Welcome to your personal journaling space! Sometimes, writing down your thoughts can feel like a task, but speaking them out makes it easier and more natural. That’s why we’ve made it simple for you to record and transcribe your thoughts effortlessly.

How It Works:

1. Click the button below to open SpeechTexter, where you can record your thoughts.
2. Once you’re done, copy the transcribed text from SpeechTexter.
3. Return to this page and click the button that links to Notion.
4. Paste your transcribed text into your journal on Notion and save your entry.
